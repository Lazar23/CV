Dr Lazar Kaluđerović

Assitant Professor at Faculty of Agriculture

I am Geologist with a Ph.D. from Belgrade University, Faculty of Mining and Geology. I have experience in conducting research, managing practical work with students, writing, and editing research papers. My field of interest is the environment, minerals, clay mineralogy, soil mineralogy, sediments, nanotechnology. My passion is also data analytics, and it amazes me the stories one can tell with properly processed data. Coming from the Research world (full of data), I have to evaluate a big amount of data and to do data visualization. In the end, I am a researcher. But at the same time, social awareness and system thinking about novel solutions are necessary. This is why I co-founded NGO "United for Better Society" with the purpose to deal with environmenatal topics.
